What is Halloweek?

Halloweek is Alternative High School's Celebration of Halloween, only this time it's for a week, with different events every day! Feel free to wear a different costume every day, or not wear a costume at all. Or you know, you could wear one costume one day. Whatever floats your boat. There will be sugar skull face painting, doughnut string eating and a costume contest. If that's not enough, keep reading below!

 

 

Sugar Skulls

Get your face painted like a traditional "Day of the Dead" sugar skull. One of our talented volunteers will paint your face with style, and grace. You will definitley have a funny face.

Doughnut String Eating Game Thing Where People Eat Donuts off Strings Without using their hands.

Costume Contest

There will be a costume contest with up to NINE chances to win. Differnet costumes can win in different categories, some of which are: Best group costume, most complex costume, best portrayal of a character, and most creative/unique costume.
